Rules of Supershow

Each player takes a turn playing a card

On your turn you can

  • Play a LEAD move
  • Play a FOLLOW UP if you have a LEAD in play
  • Play a FINISH if you have a FOLLOW UP in play

    • Repeat the steps above until someone hits a finisher and wins!!
